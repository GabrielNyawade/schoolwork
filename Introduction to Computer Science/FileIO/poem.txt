What's better?
Seeking attention for it's own sake?
Or should I reserve myself,
Having nothing to give?

Sweet words spoken from afar,
Like a sweet aroma from the neighbours',
Like a sweet melody from a distant place,
You're probably tired by now.

Is it more how than what,
How can I give rather than what?
Or am I qualifying an impossibility,
Instead of asking, "Can I?"

All these questions, complicated,
I'd rather have them unanswered,
Leave them at maybe,
One foot inside, one out,
Marinating in pretence.

It's hard, so hard,
I'd like to know the truth,
Sad as it may come,
Like bitter medicine,
For this faint heart.

If you can't heal me, kill me,
Turn my heart off,
Or change the channel,
I don't want to play another episode.
